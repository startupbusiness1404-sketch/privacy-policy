Arrow Escape requires an internet connection to access and play the game. The app may check whether an internet connection is available and may check for supported app versions to ensure users are using an up-to-date version.

The app may store basic game progress locally on your device, such as completed levels, stars, moves, hints, and similar gameplay progress. This information stays on your device and is used only to provide the game experience.

Third-Party Services

Arrow Escape may use platform services such as Google Play to distribute updates and redirect users to the app’s Google Play Store page when an update is required.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Arrow Escape does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since Arrow Escape does not require personal information or user accounts, we minimize the amount of data handled by the app. Local game progress is stored on the user’s device.
