1. Overview

Sortia: Ball Sort Puzzle is a casual puzzle game. We respect your privacy and aim to keep data use simple and transparent.

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal details to play.

2. Information We Handle

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Level progress and completed levels.
  • Coin balance and reward status.
  • Gameplay actions such as retries, undo use, and extra moves.

This local gameplay data is used to run the game experience. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, this information may be deleted.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Sortia: Ball Sort Puzzle uses Google AdMob to show ads, including banner ads, interstitial ads, app open ads, and rewarded ads.

Google AdMob may collect or receive information such as device identifiers, approximate location, app interactions, diagnostics, and advertising data to provide, measure, personalize, and improve ads according to Google's policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. Rewarded Ads and Coins

Rewarded ads are optional. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, the ad network confirms whether the reward was earned. The app then adds the selected coin reward to your local balance.

Coins are virtual in-game items. They have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for money or transferred outside the app.

5. Children and Families

The app is designed as a casual puzzle game. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review the concern.

6. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to watch rewarded ads.
  • You can manage ad personalization through your device or Google account settings.
  • You can clear local app data through your device settings.
  • You can stop all app data use by uninstalling the app.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to keep the app experience reliable. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the publisher website: startupbusiness.co.in.
