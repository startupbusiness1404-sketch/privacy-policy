Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Vaultia: Status Downloader ("Vaultia", "we", "our", or "us") helps users save media from supported platforms to their own device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Vaultia does not require account registration. The app may process links that you paste, media files that you download, local download history, and basic device permissions needed to save files to your device gallery or storage.

How We Use Information

  • To detect the platform for a pasted link.
  • To download media requested by you.
  • To show recent downloads and download history inside the app.
  • To save media to your device gallery when you choose that action.
  • To support app updates, ads, purchases, reviews, and diagnostics through third-party SDKs when enabled.

Local Storage

Downloaded files and download history are stored locally on your device. You can remove downloaded files from your device using your file manager, gallery app, or app storage controls.

Third-Party Services

Vaultia may use third-party services such as Google Mobile Ads, Google Play services, in-app purchase services, update services, and media extraction or download APIs. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Permissions

The app may request storage, media, or gallery-related permissions so it can save files to your device. Denying permissions may limit saving or export features.

Children's Privacy

Vaultia is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

User Responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to download, save, or share any media you access through the app. Please respect copyright, platform terms, and the privacy of others.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date above.
